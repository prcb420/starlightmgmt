About us





  • Starlight Management

    Founded in 2008 with a vision of integrity and quality, Starlight Management owns and operates five hotels across Utah and Nebraska. Unlike other hotel management companies, Starlight has an ownership stake in all its properties, ensuring a vested interest in their performance. This approach contributes to superior guest satisfaction, product quality, and profitability.

  • Management

    Our guiding principles are centered around three key stakeholders: our guests, our owners, and our associates. We are driven by a strong focus on revenue and sales. Our guests enjoy a consistently high level of service, enhancing their overall experience. Our owners reap the benefits of exceptional top-line results, leading to optimal efficiency. Lastly, our associates gain from steady business volumes, which align with our commitment to full-time employment.

  • INVESTMENT

    Investment strategy of Starlight Management is based on selective acquisition, development and management of hotels in major cities of Unites States. Starlight Management has successful completed transactions from 68 room limited service hotel to 200 room full service convention property. Starlight Management is committed to growing its market share and adding value to its portfolio through renovations, repositioning and expansion.

  • OUR PHILOSOPHY

    Our objective is to grow by selectively acquiring, developing, and managing upscale hotels.


    We aim to deliver a high Return On Investment (ROI) through efficient operations and surpassing brand requirements, aided by professionals with exceptional experience, training, and impeccable credentials.

  • CAREERS

    We are committed to recruiting, developing, and retaining the most professional individuals, providing them with ample opportunities to hone their skills and expand their knowledge. We are constantly on the lookout for passionate and motivated individuals to join our team.
